Anna does a wonderful job hosting and facilitating breathwork and sound bath events. I've been to many of such events and I never come away disappointed or regret the decision. The events are well structured and I enjoy not having any expectations of myself or my participation. I can go to one even in my most introverted/closed off states and leave feeling relaxed, centered, and sociable.

As a facilitator, Anna does very well creating a safe space to allow personal healing. Anna is talented with the singing bowls, and she also has a great voice for guided meditations and singing. Very calm, hypnotic, and gentle. I feel very taken care of in the space and it makes it easy for me to let go and let my body have the experience it needs to re-center. Recommend for anyone who needs a break from the stress of the day-to-day grind, recovering from burnout, or is going through a difficult stretch of life. Anna values the power and connection of a group ceremony and it shows.
